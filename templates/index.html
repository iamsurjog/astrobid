Rules of the Auction

  • Each team starts with 100,000 credits.
  • Planets are put up for auction one at a time and bid is incremented by the auctioneer.
  • The highest bidder at the end of the auction wins the planet.
  • Each planet has its own hidden value
  • In order to win, one must have at least 2 planets and highest score
  • Score is calculated by adding the profit from each planet and BeeJays left in account
